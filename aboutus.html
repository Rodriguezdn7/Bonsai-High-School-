About Us

The Bonsai Bands and Orchestras are one of the most respected music programs in the country. The programs have represented Bonsai HS at regional, state, and national events over the 90-year history of the program. We want to welcome anyone who has an interest in being a member of our programs. Please click in the various links to learn more about us.

Mission Statement

This program is designed to expose students to quality band and orchestra literature, as it relates to significant historical periods. The students will be required to perform within ensembles and as soloists. There will be in depth discussions about, but not limited to, tone quality, articulation, musicality, literature, and instrument pedagogy. Students will be exposed to professional performances on and off campus. Students that graduate from Bonsai HS will be college ready for a career in music.
